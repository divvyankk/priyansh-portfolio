About

The Story

I'm a freestyle hip-hop dancer competing and performing within India's hip-hop battle circuit. Known for raw energy and character-driven freestyle, I battle, train, and perform under the LDDLM banner — a Paris underground crew with real international pedigree, the same lineage that produced Diablo and StalaMuerte of Los Diablos De La Muerte. I bring that same street style and stage presence to every floor I step on.

The Journey

Four Years In

Year One
Picked It Up
I started dancing four years ago as a hip-hop freestyle dancer — no shortcuts, just practice, repetition, and showing up.
Next
Local Jams
I started getting into jams within India, testing what I had in front of real crowds and real cyphers.
Then
First Wins, Wider Travel
Travel started opening up for me — and so did the wins. I took battles judged by international names in the scene.
The Leap
Shirofes, Japan
I represented India on an international stage at Shirofes — one of Japan's biggest names in the battle scene.
The Crew
Joined LDDLM
That run is what brought me into LDDLM — the crew I rep to this day.
Right Now
Still Building
Four years in, and the run's still going — more floors, more battles, more reasons to watch.
